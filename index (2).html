About Dr. Daniel Eisenberg

Dr. Daniel Eisenberg, M.D., FACC, is an Interventional Cardiologist with over 40 years of experience, renowned for advancing cardiovascular care and improving patient outcomes. With a Biochemistry degree from Lawrence University and a Doctor of Medicine in Cardiology from Loyola University Chicago, he brings a wealth of knowledge to his practice.

His journey includes an internship, residency in Internal Medicine, and a Fellowship in Cardiology at USC, where he honed his skills. At Providence, Dr. Eisenberg has led initiatives to enhance care delivery and foster innovation, achieving significant improvements in quality and efficiency.

Passionate about prevention and education, he mentors aspiring physicians and presents at global conferences, leaving a lasting impact on cardiology.
